ABOUT
--------------------------------
This game was cobbled together as part of the February One Game a Month challenge.
It is a fairly basic autorunner that generates its terrain from the loaded audio track.

GAMEPLAY
--------------------------------
Collect the blue shards to stay alive, avoid the red ones. Don't hit the top or bottom.
Shooting drains your health, so shoot wisely!

CONTROLS
--------------------------------

			   W: Move up

	A: Move Left         D: Move right

			  D: Move down
				 
* Left Mouse Button: Shoot
* Mouse movement: Change turret angle
